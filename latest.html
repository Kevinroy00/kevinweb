My Thoughts on KonoSuba (Season 1);

Something actually watchable for a change. Starts out a bit weaksauce, but the memes get more hardcore the longer it goes on for. The isekai genre is a good match, because it's meant to be subversive and deconstructive. Impressive character development given the genre and tone. Goes experimental with a few of its later episodes, which works well for it and I hope to see more. An enjoyable and light-hearted watch. 6/10.
